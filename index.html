Roadmap

2026 is our year for unmatched entertainment. These five key timelines gives specificity on our intended period of launch:

  • Permits and land reservations: Winter 2022
  • Design and architectural completion: Spring 2024
  • YoS.Hotel - Our modernized and first-ever Olympics hotel in U.S. history - Summer 2025
  • Venue Map - Discover all Yosemite™ locations for entertainment: Winter 2025
  • Closing contracts: Spring 2026
